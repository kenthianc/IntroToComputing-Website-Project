Notre Dame of Tacurong College

Vision

“The Notre Dame of Tacurong College, a Catholic school, envisions to be a leading institution in Central Mindanao in terms of quality education, development programs and updated technology in consonance with its commitment to a dynamic and transformational leadership role in the society it serves.”

Mission

“The mission of Notre Dame of Tacurong College is to produce highly competent graduates imbued with Christian and desirable Filipino values, sense of social responsibility and integrity; and to assume active leadership role in the locality and academic community through relevant and responsive programs, creative organizational strategies and pastoral collaboration with the local church.”

Building a vibrant community for the personnel, students, and other stakeholders

Student Life

Student life at Notre Dame of Tacurong College is vibrant and fulfilling. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence and holistic development, students engage in a wide range of activities and opportunities that foster personal growth and learning. The school provides a supportive and inclusive environment where students and pupils can explore their interests, participate in various clubs and organizations, and develop leadership skills. Through a blend of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and a sense of community, students are empowered to thrive both academically and personally during their time at NDTC.

Recreation & Wellness

Notre Dame of Tacurong College places a strong emphasis on recreation and wellness, offering diverse recreational activities, fitness facilities, and outdoor spaces to support students' physical, mental, and emotional well-being. With sports teams, fitness centers, and wellness programs, the school fosters a healthy and active lifestyle while providing counseling services for emotional support. This commitment to wellness enhances academic performance and creates a nurturing campus culture that promotes personal growth and happiness among students.

Unity Despite Diversity

Notre Dame of Tacurong College takes great pride in fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where students and faculty from various cultural backgrounds come together to form a vibrant and harmonious community. The school offers numerous cultural events and activities, promoting understanding, respect, and empathy among its stakeholders. This multicultural atmosphere prepares students to become global citizens who appreciate and value diversity. The celebration of cultural diversity enhances the educational experience and reinforces the school's commitment to creating an inclusive and welcoming space for everyone.
