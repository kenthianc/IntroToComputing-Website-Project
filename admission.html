ADMISSION

Admission Requirements for Incoming Grade 7 Students

  • Original copy of Report Card (Form 138)
  • Photocopy of Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certificate of Academic Excellence Award for grade 7 (Ranks 1 &2)
  • 2 pcs of photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • 4 pcs of 2×2 ID Picture – colored
  • 2 pcs of Long Size Brown Envelop
  • System Generated ESC Certification (for ESC grades 8 to 10 transferees)

Admission Requirements for Incoming Grade 11 Students

  • Original Copy of Grade 10 Report Card (with Learners Reference Number (LRN) if graduted as Public Junior High School Student)
  • Photocopy of Grade 10 Report Card
  • Certificate of Junior High School Completion
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certificate of Academic Excellence Award for grade 7 (Ranks 1 &2)
  • 2 pcs of photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • 4 pcs of 2×2 ID Picture – colored
  • 2 pcs of Long Size Brown Envelop
  • System Generated ESC Certification from the Junior High School Principal(ESC Grantees only)
